## ‚úÖ THE JACOB TEST (Use This Before EVERY Post)

### BEFORE YOU POST, ASK:

1. **"Would Jacob think 'That's me' or 'This guy gets it'?"**
   - YES = Post it
   - NO = Rewrite

2. **"Am I SHOWING his experience or TELLING him what to do?"**
   - Showing = Good
   - Telling = Bad

3. **"Am I WITH Jacob or ABOVE Jacob?"**
   - With (brother) = Good
   - Above (guru) = Bad

4. **"Is this 100% empathy with 0% solutions?"**
   - YES = Good for Stage 1
   - NO = Save for later stages

5. **"Would I have stopped scrolling to read this when I was stuck?"**
   - YES = Post it
   - NO = Rewrite

6. **"Can Jacob see himself in SPECIFIC details?"**
   - YES = Specific enough
   - NO = Too vague

7. **"Does it end with reflection, not a sales pitch?"**
   - YES = Good
   - NO = Remove the CTA
