PHASE 6: RELATIONSHIPS

Your default mode doesn't just sabotage you, it destroys your relationships. This phase exposes how your unconscious patterns have damaged your connections with women and male friends, and how to rebuild them with your new awareness.

FROM MY EXPERIENCE:

I was a chameleon. Different person with different people. The funny guy with some friends, the serious guy with others. The agreeable version around my wife, the tough version around certain men. I morphed myself to fit whatever the situation required. I thought I was being adaptive, socially intelligent. The truth? I was terrified of being rejected for who I actually was.

Then I got exhausted. Keeping track of all the versions, remembering who I was supposed to be around each person, it was a full-time job. So I tried something terrifying: I showed up as myself. Same guy, every situation. Some people didn't like it. Some relationships ended. But the ones that stayed? Those became real. That's when I learned, you can't have authentic connections while wearing a mask.
