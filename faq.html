How long does it take to see results?

Most men see significant changes within the first few weeks. The 3-month package is designed for lasting transformation, but you'll notice improvements in confidence and clarity from day one.

Here's what you can expect:

  • Week 1-2: Clarity on what's holding you back
  • Week 3-4: First actionable steps and momentum
  • Month 2-3: Significant life changes and new habits
  • Ongoing: Continued growth and transformation

What happens in a mentoring session?

Each 50-minute session is focused on your specific challenges. We identify what's holding you back, create actionable steps, and I hold you accountable between sessions.

A typical session includes:

  • Check-in: Review progress since last session
  • Deep dive: Explore current challenges and obstacles
  • Strategy: Create specific action steps
  • Accountability: Set clear expectations for next session

Do you work with men outside Denmark?

Yes! I work with men worldwide via video calls. My approach works regardless of location - transformation happens within you, not in a specific place.

I've worked with men from:

  • United States, Canada, and Mexico
  • United Kingdom, Germany, and other European countries
  • Australia, New Zealand, and Asia
  • And many other countries worldwide

All sessions are conducted via Zoom or similar platforms, so location is never a barrier to transformation.

What if I'm not sure about committing to 3 months?

Start with a single session ($250) to see if we're a good fit. Many men choose this option first, then upgrade to the package once they experience the results.

Here's my recommendation:

  • Discovery Call (Free): 30-minute chat to see if we connect
  • Single Session ($250): One powerful session to experience my approach
  • 3-Month Package ($2,700): Complete transformation journey

There's no pressure to commit long-term. Start small and see the difference one session can make.

How do I know if I'm ready for mentoring?

If you're feeling stuck, overwhelmed, or know there's more to life than what you're currently experiencing, you're ready. The fact that you're asking this question shows you're already taking the first step.

You might be ready if you:

  • Feel like you're going through the motions without purpose
  • Know you're capable of more but don't know how to get there
  • Are tired of repeating the same patterns and getting the same results
  • Want to break free from limiting beliefs or past experiences
  • Feel ready to take responsibility for your life and happiness

Remember: You don't need to have it all figured out. That's what I'm here for.

What makes your approach different from other mentors?

I've lived through addiction, cancer, and rock bottom. I don't just teach theory - I share real experiences and proven strategies that worked for me. Plus, I'm committed to your success, not just paid sessions.

What sets me apart:

  • Real Experience: I've been where you are - I know what it feels like
  • Proven Results: My methods have worked for me and hundreds of other men
  • Authentic Connection: I'm not a corporate coach - I'm a real person who cares
  • Ongoing Support: I'm invested in your long-term success, not just sessions
  • No BS Approach: I tell you the truth, even when it's hard to hear

I'm not here to sell you a dream. I'm here to help you build the life you actually want.
