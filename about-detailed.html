This Is My Story

I know what it's like to feel completely broken. To wake up every morning hating yourself. To feel like you're living someone else's life.

Growing Up in Fear

My father was an alcoholic who terrorized our home. I learned to walk on eggshells, never feeling safe in my own house. This shaped everything about how I saw myself and the world.

14 Years of Running

From 14 to 28, I used drugs to escape. Anything to numb the pain, to not feel the brokenness inside. I was running from myself, but I couldn't outrun the truth.

Cancer Hit Me Twice

Life forced me to face reality. Twice. Each diagnosis was a wake-up call that I couldn't keep destroying myself. I had to choose: die or change.

Confident & At Peace

Now I'm confident, in peace, and aiming to be a better person every day. I'm not afraid to be egotistic in order to keep my peace. I've earned this confidence through my journey.
