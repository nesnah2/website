Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me

How long does it take to see results?

Most men see significant changes within the first few weeks. The 3-month program is designed for lasting transformation, but you'll notice improvements in confidence and clarity from day one.

Here's what you can expect:

  • Week 1-2: Clarity on what's holding you back
  • Week 3-4: First actionable steps and momentum
  • Month 2-3: Significant life changes and new habits
  • Ongoing: Continued growth and transformation

What happens in a coaching session?

Each 50-minute session is focused on your specific challenges. We identify what's holding you back, create actionable steps, and I hold you accountable between sessions.

A typical session includes:

  • Check-in: Review progress since last session
  • Deep dive: Explore current challenges and obstacles
  • Strategy: Create specific action steps
  • Accountability: Set clear expectations for next session

Do you work with men outside Denmark?

Yes! I work exclusively online with men worldwide via video calls. I've successfully helped men from every continent.

My approach works regardless of location because true transformation happens within you, not in a specific place. All sessions are conducted online via Zoom or similar platforms.

This means you can work with me from anywhere in the world, at any time that fits your schedule. Location is never a barrier to transformation.

What if I'm not sure about committing to 3 months?

Start with a single session ($250) to see if we're a good fit. Many men choose this option first, then upgrade to the program once they experience the results.

Here's my recommendation:

  • Discovery Call (Free): 30-minute chat to see if we connect
  • Single Session ($250): One powerful session to experience my approach
  • 3-Month Program ($2,700): Complete transformation journey

There's no pressure to commit long-term. Start small and see the difference one session can make.

What makes your approach different?

I don't give you answers. I ask the questions that help you find your own truth. Because real transformation only happens when you discover it yourself.

My approach is built on:

  • Truth First: Brutal honesty about where you actually are
  • No Judgment: I've been where you are - you can tell me anything
  • Action Over Theory: Every session ends with concrete steps
  • Real Accountability: I won't watch you sabotage yourself

How do I book a session?

It's simple. Click "Book Free Call" anywhere on the site, or email me directly at contact@mikkelhansen.org.

For the free discovery call, you can book directly through the calendar. For paid sessions, I'll send you a payment link after we confirm the time.

I respond to all inquiries within 24 hours, usually much faster.
