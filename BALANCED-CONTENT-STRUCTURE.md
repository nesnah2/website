# HOW TO MENTION PAIN WITHOUT DWELLING ON IT

---

## **THE STRUCTURE:**

### **MENTION PAIN BRIEFLY ‚Üí FOCUS ON TRANSFORMATION**

**NOT:**
- Long story about the pain
- Detailed description of suffering
- Heavy emotional weight

**YES:**
- Quick mention ("I used to...")
- Then immediately pivot to transformation
- Focus on where you are NOW

---

## **THE FORMULA:**

**"I used to [pain].**
**Now I [transformation].**
**Not because [external thing changed].**
**Because [I did the work]."**

---

**‚ùå TOO MUCH PAIN:**

---

## **THE RULE:**

### **MENTION PAIN IN 1-2 SENTENCES MAX**

**Then immediately pivot to:**
- Where you are NOW
- What changed
- What you learned
- The transformation

---

## **STRUCTURE TEMPLATE:**

**1. Quick pain mention (1-2 sentences)**
"I used to [pain]."

**2. Transformation (2-3 sentences)**
"Now I [transformation]."

**3. Why it changed (1 sentence)**
"Not because [external]. Because [internal work]."

**4. Question (1 sentence)**
"What changed for you?"
