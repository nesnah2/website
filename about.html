My Story

From the shadows of addiction to guiding men into their authentic power. This is my real journey.

I've Been Where You Are

I lived in the shadows of my soul. Scared and wounded. Always feeling outside and alone, trying my best to act my way into being accepted.

My Father's Shadow

My father drank and terrorized me. I grew up in fear, never feeling safe in my own home.

14 Years of Addiction

From 14 to 28, I was a drug addict. I used anything to numb the pain, to escape the reality I couldn't face.

Cancer Twice

Life hit me with cancer twice. Each time, I had to face my own mortality and the reality of what I was doing to myself.

My Family

Now I have 4 kids that I love. They're my why. My reason to be better, to heal, to grow.

I Know These Struggles Deeply

Self-Esteem

Feeling like you're not enough, never measuring up to some impossible standard.

Loneliness

Feeling disconnected, even when surrounded by people. Like you're on the outside looking in.

Addiction

Any kind of addiction - drugs, alcohol, work, porn, food. Using something to numb the pain.

Feeling Wrong

Feeling like there's something fundamentally broken inside you. Like you don't belong.

How I Work

I'm not here to give you answers. I'm here to ask the right questions that help you find your own truth.

Truth-Based Growth

Any real growth is based in the truth of the present. We start where you actually are, not where you think you should be.

Vulnerability as Strength

It's okay to be vulnerable as a man. In fact, it's necessary. Real strength comes from being real about your struggles.

Trust & No Judgment

I create a room for trust and no judgment. You can be completely honest about where you are.

Ready to Start Your Transformation?

If my story resonates with you, if you're tired of living in the shadows, let's talk. Real transformation is possible.
