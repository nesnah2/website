Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see results?

Most men see significant changes within the first few weeks. The 3-month package is designed for lasting transformation, but you'll notice improvements in confidence and clarity from day one.

What happens in a mentoring session?

Each 50-minute session is focused on your specific challenges. We identify what's holding you back, create actionable steps, and I hold you accountable between sessions.

Do you work with men outside Denmark?

Yes! I work with men worldwide via video calls. My approach works regardless of location - transformation happens within you, not in a specific place.

What if I'm not sure about committing to 3 months?

Start with a single session ($250) to see if we're a good fit. Many men choose this option first, then upgrade to the package once they experience the results.
