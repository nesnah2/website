About Me

I've Been Where You Are

Behind a perfect-looking childhood were chaos and a father who drank himself to death. I learned to perform, to be what others wanted. I used every escape available—work, relationships, substances—to avoid facing myself.

Cancer hit me twice. Alone in a hospital bed, I finally faced the truth: I was running from everything. That's when I discovered my default mode—the automatic patterns that had been running my life without permission.

Why I Do This Work

I mentor men because I know what it feels like to be stuck. I know the shame and confusion when something feels wrong but you can't identify it. I know what it's like to look good on the outside while dying inside.

Most importantly, I know what it takes to break free. It's not about positive thinking or quick fixes. It's about facing your default mode patterns and choosing a different path.

My Credentials & Experience

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions men ask before starting their transformation journey

What kind of men do you work with?

Men who are ready to drop the mask, face themselves, and create real change. You don't need to have it all together—just a willingness to be honest and show up. I work with men who are feeling stuck, lost, or disconnected from their authentic selves.

What results can I expect?

Clarity, confidence, and a renewed sense of purpose. You'll learn to trust yourself, speak your truth, and build a life that feels real—on your terms. Most men experience greater self-awareness, improved relationships, and a stronger sense of direction in their lives.

Is this therapy?

No. This is not therapy or counseling. It's direct, practical mentoring focused on presence, action, and transformation—man to man. While therapy often focuses on healing past wounds, mentoring focuses on building your future and creating the life you want to live.

How do I get started?

Just reach out. We'll have a real conversation—no pressure, no sales pitch. If it feels right, we'll take the next step together. You can email me directly or book a free discovery call to see if we're a good fit.

What if I'm not sure if this is right for me?

That's exactly why I offer a free discovery call. We'll spend 30 minutes talking about your situation, what you're looking for, and whether we're a good fit. There's no pressure to commit—it's just a conversation between two men.

What makes your approach different?

I've been where you are. I understand the struggle because I lived it. This isn't theory—it's real experience. I focus on practical, actionable steps rather than just talking about problems. We work on breaking down your default mode patterns and building new ways of being.
