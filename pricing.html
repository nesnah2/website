Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the programs and investment

What's the difference between the packages?

The Discovery Package is for men who want to understand their patterns and get a roadmap. The Transformation Package is for men ready to actively break down old patterns and build new ones. The Mastery Package is for men committed to deep, lasting change and ongoing support.

How do I know which package is right for me?

Start with the free discovery call. We'll discuss your situation, goals, and commitment level. I'll recommend the package that best fits where you are and what you're ready for. You can always upgrade later.

What if I can't afford the full package upfront?

I offer payment plans for the Transformation and Mastery packages. We can discuss options during the discovery call. The investment is significant, but so is the transformation.

How long does it take to see results?

Most men start feeling shifts within the first few weeks of the Discovery Package. Real transformation happens over months, not days. The longer packages provide the time and support needed for lasting change.

What if I'm not satisfied with the program?

I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not getting value from our work together after the first session, I'll refund your investment. Your transformation is my priority.

Can I upgrade from a smaller package?

Absolutely. Many men start with the Discovery Package and then upgrade to Transformation or Mastery. We can discuss this at any time during our work together.
