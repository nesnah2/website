Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during a session?

Each session is tailored to your specific needs, but generally follows this structure: We start by identifying where you're currently stuck, explore the patterns keeping you there, and develop practical strategies to help you stop running and start living fully in your present. The focus is always on actionable steps you can implement immediately.

How are sessions conducted?

All sessions are conducted online via video call (Zoom). This allows us to connect regardless of your location, while still maintaining the personal connection needed for this deep work. You'll receive a private link before each session.

What's your refund policy?

For the 3-Month Transformation Program, I offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee on your first session. If you feel it's not the right fit after our first session, I'll refund your entire payment. For single sessions, I don't offer refunds, but I do guarantee you'll receive value - if you don't, let me know and we'll figure out a solution together.

How do I know if this is right for me?

That's exactly why I offer the free discovery call. This gives us both a chance to see if we're a good fit. During this call, you can ask questions, share your situation, and get a feel for my approach. I'll be honest if I don't think I can help you, and may suggest alternatives if that's the case.
